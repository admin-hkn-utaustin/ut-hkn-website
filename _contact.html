HKN Mailing Lists

We maintain a number of mailing lists to help our members stay in contact.

Officers Want to contact the HKN leadership? Send an email to us at hkn_contact@utlists.utexas.edu.
Actives Are you a current HKN member at UT Austin? Please contact an officer to be placed on the Actives mailing list.
Alumni Alumni of HKN at UT Austin can join the alumni mailing list or the Facebook group to receive announcements and updates from us. For questions or comments, please email the Alumni Chair Amanda Akin or another officer.
Pledges Each semester we place all of our pledges on the pledges mailing list in order to communicate with them during the pledge process. Please contact the recording secretary to be added to the mailing list.
