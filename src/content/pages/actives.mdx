### Requirements for Being an Active

To be considered active in HKN, you must complete the following each semester:

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Undergraduate Requirements">

- **Three** general meetings
- **Two** social events
- **One** society event (Peer Advising Night, Sig Party)
- **One** Fireside or Tech Talk

</Tab>

<Tab name="Graduate Requirements">

- **One** general meeting
- **One** social event
- **One** other event (Peer Advising Night, Sig Party, Fireside, Tech Talk)

</Tab>

</Tabs>

### Benefits of Being an Active

- Access to the HKN office
- Wear HKN stole during graduation
  - Must be active during your graduating semester
- Interact with new pledges and other members as a tight-knit community
- Free food at meetings
- Ability to become an officer
